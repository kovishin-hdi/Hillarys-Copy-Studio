## Five key principles at play

1. **Right Source for Right Stage** — every stage has a dominant source logic.
2. **Convenience Leads** — 80% of all communication should reduce friction.
3. **Real Over Perfect** — use real India context to build trust and relatability.
4. **AI Multiplies, Humans Direct** — AI creates scale; humans add judgment and emotion.
5. **Trust Converts** — reassurance content drives action at the final mile.
