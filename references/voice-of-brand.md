# Hillarys India — Voice of Brand

Version 1.0  
Internal Brand Communication Framework  
For Marketing, CRM, CX, Design Consultants, Sales & Content Teams

---

# 1. Brand Foundation

Hillarys India is not a curtains and blinds company.

Hillarys is a guided home styling experience brand.

We help homeowners transform their spaces through beautifully designed window solutions delivered through an effortless, premium, end-to-end service experience.

Our brand exists to remove stress, confusion, and fragmentation from the window styling journey.

We do not sell products.  
We deliver confidence, comfort, transformation, and convenience.

# 7. Language Principles

## 1. Simplify

Customers should never feel overwhelmed.

### Bad:
“Explore our extensive portfolio of advanced window treatment systems.”

### Good:
“Explore curtains and blinds designed beautifully for your home.”

---

## 2. Guide

We help customers feel supported.

### Bad:
“Please share your measurements.”

### Good:
“Our team will guide you through measurements and product selection.”

---

## 3. Reassure

Reduce uncertainty at every stage.

### Bad:
“We will update you soon.”

### Good:
“We’ll keep you updated throughout the process to ensure a smooth experience.”

---

## 4. Humanise

Always sound human.

### Bad:
“Ticket raised successfully.”

### Good:
“We’ve received your request and our team is already looking into it.”

---

## 5. Speak Emotionally, Not Technically

Focus on outcomes, not mechanisms.

### Bad:
“Motorised dual-layer blinds.”

### Good:
“Light control and privacy at the touch of a button.”

# 11. Writing Style Rules

- Short, clear sentences
- Conversational professionalism

- Excess punctuation

- Lowest price
- Discount blinds

- Best rates
- Limited stock

We do not aggressively ask for referrals.

Preferred framing:

“Share the Hillarys experience.”

Not:

“Refer now and earn rewards.”
